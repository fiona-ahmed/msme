Our Story

A journey of discovery, innovation, and the relentless pursuit of Korean skincare excellence

Experience Korean Innovation

Where Korean Science Meets Beauty

msme skin was born from discovering niche Korean skincare ingredients not widely used in the West and bringing them to a global audience. We formulated with powerful actives like Volufiline, Centella Asiatica, and advanced Korean peptides, carefully encapsulating them with specially selected penetration enhancers to ensure these ingredients reached deeper into the skin for maximum efficacy.

Our mission was clear: deliver luxury Korean skincare without charging the hundreds of dollars that other brands with the same formulas demand. In a world saturated with empty promises and marketing hype, we decided to take a different path.

Our founder, a passionate advocate for evidence-based beauty, spent years researching the latest advances in Korean cosmetic chemistry alongside leading chemists and research scientists. Every formula begins in our laboratory, where we meticulously select and test ingredients based on peer-reviewed research and clinical efficacy from Korea's most innovative skincare institutes.

Ingredient Intelligence

We believe in ingredient transparency and clinical validation. Each active component in our formulations is chosen for its proven efficacy and safety profile.

Take our signature Plumping Lip Balm: featuring Volufiline and Adifyline - two clinically-proven peptides that have been shown in studies to increase lip volume by up to 8.3% within 28 days. Adifyline studies specifically demonstrated a 15% improvement in lip contour definition within just 14 days of application. This isn't marketing speak; this is science.

Our founding team saw these remarkable results firsthand during using early formulas on themselves, friends and willing volunteers and decided to create this brand to help people achieve natural enhancement without invasive procedures. We source the finest natural ingredients like pure honey, not just for their luxurious feel, but for their documented benefits to skin health and barrier function.
